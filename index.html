About Us

HISTORY

Do you know if there’s a roadside drink stall in Kemaman town? It’s not your usual drinks menu, you know! The taste is so unique that once you try it, it might linger in your dreams. Feel free to drop by our small stall! We started operating in July 2021. Initially, we only served coffee, and a year later, we shifted our focus to food items. We emphasize on the presentation of our beverages for aesthetic impact and keep up with the latest trends. Additionally, we concentrate on incorporating local flavors to create a unique menu. Our location by the roadside makes it convenient for customers to drive by and order drinks or food
